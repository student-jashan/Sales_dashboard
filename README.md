# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The **Supermarket Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across multiple regions, customer segments, product categories, and states. It enables users to monitor key business metrics and identify sales trends through intuitive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze sales by category and sub-category.
- Compare customer segments.
- Identify high-performing states.
- Track customer purchase behavior.
- Enable interactive filtering for deeper analysis.

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1. Sales by Category
A bar chart comparing total sales across product categories.

Categories include:
- Technology
- Furniture
- Office Supplies

**Insight:** Technology generates the highest sales, followed by Furniture.

---

### 2. Segment Analysis

A pie chart showing sales contribution by customer segment.

Segments:
- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

**Insight:** Consumer customers contribute the largest share of total sales.

---

### 3. Average Sales by State

Horizontal bar chart displaying average sales across different states.

States include:
- Wyoming
- Nevada
- Montana
- Washington
- California
- Utah
- Idaho

**Insight:** Wyoming has the highest average sales among the displayed states.

---

### 4. Sales by Sub-Category

Treemap visualization representing sales distribution among product sub-categories.

Major sub-categories:
- Copiers
- Machines
- Tables
- Chairs
- Bookcases
- Phones
- Storage
- Accessories
- Appliances
- Supplies

**Insight:** Copiers contribute the highest sales.

---

### 5. Customer Sales Table

Detailed table containing:

- Customer Name
- Customer Segment
- State
- Total Orders
- Total Sales

This helps identify high-value customers and their purchasing patterns.

---

## 🎛 Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes slicers for:

- Region
  - Central
  - East
  - South
  - West

- Ship Mode
  - Standard Class
  - Second Class
  - Same Day
  - First Class

These filters allow users to dynamically analyze sales across different dimensions.

---

## 🔍 Business Insights

- Technology is the highest-performing product category.
- Consumer customers generate the largest portion of revenue.
- Copiers are the top-selling sub-category.
- Wyoming records the highest average sales among selected states.
- Interactive filters make it easy to explore regional and shipping trends.
